Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book in to get a gym program?

A: You can book at main reception or gym desk on main gym floor in person or by phone

Q: How much do classes cost?

A: Up to four classes a month are included in the standard membership. Additional classes cost €5. Gold members have unlimited number of classes per month!

Q: Do you offer personal training sessions?

A: Yes, we offer personal training sessions, charged at an additional cost. Prices vary depending on trainer but do not exceed €30 per hour

Q: How do I book in to fitness classes?

A: All classes can be booked through reception. Please have your membership details at hand

Q: Is secure parking available in the vicinity?

A: Secure, and free for all members, private parking in front of Booterstown Fitness is available during opening hours
